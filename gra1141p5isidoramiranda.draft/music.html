    Did You Know That There’s a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd Released in March 2023, Did You Know That There’s a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd is Lana Del Rey’s ninth studio album, blending introspective lyricism with cinematic production. The album explores themes of memory, family, love, loss, and self-reflection through songs like “The Grants,” which honors her relatives, and the haunting title track that contemplates being forgotten. “A&W” stands out for its experimental sound and raw honesty, while “Fingertips” offers a stream-of-consciousness look into her inner life. Other tracks, such as “Candy Necklace,” “Paris, Texas,” and “Taco Truck x VB,” mix melancholy storytelling with nostalgic references, creating one of her most personal and emotionally layered works.
