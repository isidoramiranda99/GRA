Summary

Spirited Away is an Academy Award-winning animated fantasy film by Hayao Miyazaki about a sullen 10-year-old girl named Chihiro who gets trapped in a magical spirit world after her parents transform into pigs. To survive and free her parents, she must work in a bathhouse for gods and spirits, confronting a host of fantastical creatures and the sorceress Yubaba.
